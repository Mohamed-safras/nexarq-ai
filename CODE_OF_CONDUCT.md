# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in Nexarq a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Behavior that is not acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments in any form
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling or sustained disruptive behavior
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening a private issue or emailing the maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct may be removed from the project team.
